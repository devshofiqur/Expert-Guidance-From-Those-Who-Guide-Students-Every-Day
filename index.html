The Mission Behind Sentra

In schools across the globe, dedicated counselors wake up every morning with one mission: to help students discover their potential and navigate the path to their dreams.

These professionals have years—sometimes decades—of experience. They've read thousands of applications, guided countless students through difficult decisions, and celebrated every acceptance letter as if it were their own.

But their impact was limited by geography. Until now.

Sentra exists to break down those walls. We've created a space where experienced school counselors can extend their reach, sharing their hard-earned wisdom with students who need it most—regardless of where they live.

This is more than a platform. It's a movement to democratize access to world-class guidance.

Our Quality Promise

Only Top-Performing Counselors Stay Active

We continuously monitor counselor performance to ensure you receive exceptional guidance every time. Our automated quality system maintains the highest standards.

Quality Threshold: 3.5/5

Every session is rated on three criteria: Guidance Quality, Clarity & Helpfulness, and Professionalism. Counselors must maintain an average of 3.5 or higher.

Student ratings drive accountability

Automatic Suspension

Counselors who receive 3 consecutive ratings below 3.5 are automatically suspended for 30 days. This ensures consistent quality and protects students.

Zero tolerance for poor quality

Smart Weighted Ratings

Overall ratings use a weighted system: 70% from recent 10 sessions, 30% from older sessions. This rewards improvement and current performance.

Recent performance matters most
